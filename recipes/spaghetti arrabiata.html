Spaghetti Arrabiata

Description

Spaghetti Arrabbiata ( all’arrabbiata) which translates to”angry” – is one my favorite pasta dishes to make. The sauce is made with just couple of ingredients: sweet cherry tomatoes, garlic, good quality olive oil, and spiced with crushed red peppers and fresh basil. This recipe calls for 20 garlic cloves and the sauce makes a large amount. If I am not feeding a crowd, I will store the sauce in the fridge for up to 3 days and use to make various delicious weeknight meals.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 20 garlic cloves smashed
  • 2 (24 ounces) packets of cherry tomatoes
  • 7 ounces organic tomato paste
  • 1/3 cup chopped fresh basil leaves, plus extra for serving
  • 1 tbsp red crushed pepper
  • black pepper, to taste
  • salt, to taste
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup pasta water
  • 1&1/2 pound Spaghetti (for 1-3 people)
  • freshly grated parmesan for garnish

Steps

  1. Prepare the Pasta
  2. Cook the Garlic and Shallots
  3. Add the tomatoes
  4. Make the sauce
  5. Add fresh basil
  6. Combine with pasta
  7. Serve
