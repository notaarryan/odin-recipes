White Sauce for Pasta

Description

White Sauce Pasta is simply cooked pasta mixed with a silky smooth & decadent white sauce made of milk, butter and flour. It is also known as Bechamel Sauce has its origin from the Italian & French Cuisines. In classic culinary terms, this is what is known as a mother sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons margarine
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon, crumbled
  • 1&1/2 cups boiling water
  • 1 cup 2% milk
  • ground white pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Make the Roux (Flour paste)
  2. Prepare the Sauce
  3. Season the Sauce
  4. Combine with Pasta
  5. Serve
