Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, pasta dish of Italian origin, made with broad often ruffled noodles and a tomato or white sauce. Lasagna, in the singular, is a southern Italian variation of what northern Italians call lasagne, in the plural.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • A can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 table spoons of white sugar
  • Fresh parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
